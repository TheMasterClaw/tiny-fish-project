**Theme**: AI × Blockchain for Good  
**Focus**: Marine Conservation

## 🌊 What It Does

CoralGuard combines **AI computer vision** with **blockchain** to monitor and protect coral reefs worldwide.

### The Problem
- Coral reefs dying at alarming rates
- No centralized monitoring system
- Conservation efforts underfunded
- Data lacks transparency/trust

### The Solution
- **AI Reef Analysis** - Computer vision assesses reef health from photos
- **Blockchain Tracking** - Immutable reef health records
- **NFT Fundraising** - Support conservation through collectible NFTs
- **Guardian Network** - Community-driven reef monitoring

                   │
